## â“ Common Questions

**Q: Do I need internet?**
A: No! Works completely offline after installation.

**Q: What if phone dies?**
A: Data is lost. Keep phone charged or use backup device.

**Q: Can I edit after signing?**
A: No. Once truck is signed off, it's locked.

**Q: Where does the PDF go?**
A: Downloads to your device's download folder.

**Q: Can multiple people use same app?**
A: Yes, but one device per job. Data resets after completion.

**Q: How do I start a new job?**
A: After completing a job, app asks if you want to start new one.
